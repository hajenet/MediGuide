MediGuide global roadmap

Plans by version and access tier

MediGuide starts as a free multilingual nursing terminology quiz and can expand into paid question packs, analytics, and advanced anatomy interactions after the MVP is validated.

Access Tiers

Free

Starter

For first-time users and public launch validation.

  • English and Korean UI
  • Core medical abbreviations
  • Basic quiz mode
  • Wrong-answer review
  • Local progress storage
Paid

Premium

For users who need deeper practice and structured review.

  • Expanded question packs
  • Category exams
  • Spaced repetition review
  • Weakness analytics
  • Offline PWA support
Paid

Classroom

For instructors, schools, and small cohorts.

  • Custom question sets
  • Class progress reports
  • Private study links
  • Instructor review workflow
  • License-based access

Policy Notes

  • MVP content should avoid copyrighted exam questions and unlicensed anatomy images.
  • NCLEX, HESI, TEAS, and similar names should not imply official affiliation.
  • Medical content must be framed as educational, not medical advice.
  • Paid tiers should launch only after content quality, translation quality, and user demand are validated.
